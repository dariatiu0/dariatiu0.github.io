Bodily Autonomy

What is It?

Bodily Autonomy is the right to decide what to do with your body.

It is the ability to choose if you want to have sex.

It is the ability to choose who you want to have sex with.

It is the ability to choose if you want to be pregnant.

It is the ability to choose if you want to take birth control.

It is the ability to choose your partner, or if you want to have one.

It is the ability to make decisions pertaining to your body whether sexual, medical, or other.

Why is bodily autonomy important?

Having the freedom to make choices over your body empowers many, making people less susceptible to sexual assault which is the first step in ending sexual violence.

Who has bodily autonomy?

Long story short, everyone should have bodily autonomy. However, some people, such as men, have more bodily autonomy. In todays world, bodily autonomy is under attack for women, non-binary people, and trans people. Many minorities do not have access to sexual education and resources that allow them to choose what happens to their bodies. Right now, with the overturn of Roe V Wade, people may not have access to abortion clinics, birth control, and a multitude of other important resources. America's government is limiting the options that women and many other people have. This creates a lack of bodily autonomy. People are not allowed to govern their bodies. Governments are controlling this right. They wont stop with abortion and birth control access either. Supreme Court Justice Clarence Thomas implied that other established, fundamental rights would be reconsidered in the future - including a ban on same-sex marriage. By banning right to choose who you want to marry eliminates the right to choose. Aka the lack of bodily autonomy.

Bodily autonomy is under attack in America. Pregnant people, menstruating people, people who have sex, LGBTQ people, and people who just want to choose what happens to their body are being silenced and controlled. Visit the Resources and Take Action page to learn what you can do.
